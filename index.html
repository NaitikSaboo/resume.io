Resume tips for Indian freshers and job seekers

A great resume is your first impression with Indian recruiters. Keep these tips in mind:

  • Keep your resume to 1 page if you have under 5 years of experience
  • Always include your 10th and 12th percentage or CGPA
  • Add your LinkedIn profile URL — most Indian recruiters check it
  • Use action words like "Led", "Built", "Increased", "Managed"
  • List your technical skills clearly — especially for IT and engineering roles
